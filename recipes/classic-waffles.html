Classic Waffles

Description

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Ingredients note: Original recipe yields 5 servings.

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar; set aside. Pre-heat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a pre-heated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.

Helpful tip: Put hot leftover waffles on a paper towel so they don't get soggy.
